Audio System

Sound is critical to any training program. If you don’t have a good speaker system in place, people will not be able to hear the directions that are given out or any speeches that are made. You may also not have sound that’s loud enough or end up with something that sounds muddled and muddy, turning people off from your event and making them remember the negative aspects of your occasion rather than the positive moments or the reasons why you held the event in the first place.
